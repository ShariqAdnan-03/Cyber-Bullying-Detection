How Do Crowd-Users Express Their Opinions Against Software Applications in Social Media? A Fine-Grained Classification Approach

App
Review.



About

App stores allow users to search, download, and purchase software applications to accomplish daily tasks. Also, they enable crowd-users to submit textual feedback or star ratings to the downloaded software apps based on their satisfaction. Recently, crowd-user feedback contains critical information for software developers, including new features, issues, non-functional requirements, etc.


The software app market is highly competitive and constantly growing. Crowd-users can search for, download, and purchase software apps through various application stores, such as Amazon, Google Play, and Apple Stores, to fulfill their daily needs and tasks.


ML algorithms, such as MNB, LR, RF, MLP, KNN, AdaBoost, and Voting Classifier, are used to identify the associated emotions with each captured issue by processing the annotated end-user data set. We obtained satisfactory classification results, with MLP and RF classifiers having 82% and 80% average accuracies, respectively.
